Recurring Events

📅 Date: Every Wednesday

🕛 Time: 10 AM to 12 PM

📍 Location: 721 E Fairhaven, Burlington, WA 98233

Join us every week at the American Legion Memorial Post 91 for our lively Social/Coffee & Donut Hour! Enjoy delicious donuts and fresh coffee while connecting with fellow members and the community in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. It's the perfect opportunity to catch up with old friends, make new ones, and stay engaged with the vibrant life of our post. Don't miss out on this delightful weekly tradition!

📅 Date: Every 1st & 3rd Wednesday of the Month

🕛 Time: 11:30 AM to 12:30 PM

📍 Location: 721 E Fairhaven, Burlington, WA 98233

Join us at the American Legion Memorial Post 91 for Free Lunches for Veterans, held every 1st and 3rd Wednesday of the month. This is a wonderful opportunity for veterans to enjoy a complimentary meal, share stories, and connect with fellow servicemen and women. Our dedicated team is honored to serve those who have served our country. We look forward to welcoming you and showing our appreciation for your dedication and sacrifice.

📅 Date: First Saturday of the Month

🕛 Time: 10 AM

📍 Location: 721 E Fairhaven, Burlington, WA 98233

Join us for our monthly Auxiliary meetings to connect with like-minded community members and make a positive impact together! Whether you're passionate about volunteering or looking to get more involved, our meetings offer a welcoming space for collaboration and growth.

For more information or to express interest in joining the Auxiliary, please contact Lynn Handy at 360-708-7711 or Sue Whittle at 360-770-5253. We look forward to seeing you there!
