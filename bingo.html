Welcome to Bingo Night at American Legion Memorial Post 91

Every Tuesday at 6 PM

Dear Bingo Enthusiasts, we want to inform you that our beloved Bingo Night will have its last session for the season on, June 25, 2024. Its been an incredible journey filled with laughter, excitement, and fantastic prizes, and we are immensely grateful for your participation and support. But don’t worry, this isn’t goodbye—it’s just a short break! Bingo Night will resume with even more thrilling games and amazing prizes in the first week of September 2024. Mark your calendars and get ready for a spectacular comeback! Until then, enjoy your summer, and we can’t wait to see you back in September for more Bingo fun. Thank you for being a part of our Bingo community!
