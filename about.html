About American Legion Memorial Post 91

Welcome to American Legion Post 91 located in Burlington, Washington. We are dedicated to serving veterans and their families, as well as our local community.

Our post offers various programs and services aimed at supporting veterans, promoting patriotism, and assisting those in need. Whether it's through our advocacy efforts, social events, or community service projects, we strive to make a positive impact.

Join us in honoring and supporting those who have served our country and helping to build a stronger, more connected community.
